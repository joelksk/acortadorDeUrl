What is Linkly and How Does It Work?

Linkly is a simple yet powerful tool for shortening URLs and making long links easier to share. With just a few clicks, you can transform a long link into a short, easy-to-remember one. Additionally, by signing up, you'll gain access to advanced features that help you manage your links more efficiently.

Key Features:

  • Instant Link Shortening: Any long URL can be converted into a shorter one, optimized for sharing on social media, emails, and other platforms.
  • Click Tracking: Get real-time statistics on how many people have clicked on your links. You can see the number of clicks, user locations, and other relevant data.
  • URL History: If you sign up, you’ll have access to a complete history of all the links you've created. Never lose track of your links again.
  • URL Editing: Quickly and easily modify or delete links whenever you need to.
  • Custom URLs: If you're a registered user, you can personalize the final part of your URLs to make them easier to identify and share.
