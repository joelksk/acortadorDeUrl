Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 15, 2024

1. Introduction

This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect user information when using our URL shortening service. By using our service, you agree to the terms set forth in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: We collect usernames upon registration. We do not store real names or sensitive personal information.
URL Data: We store original and shortened URLs, as well as information on how many times a shortened URL is accessed.
Session Data: We may store information about your device, IP address, and session details when you use our service, though this is not directly associated with your personal identity.

3. Use of Information

The information we collect is used to:
- Provide the URL shortening service.
- Analyze the performance of shortened URLs (e.g., number of clicks).
- Protect our service from malicious or fraudulent activities.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We do not currently use cookies for the operation of the service but reserve the right to do so in the future to enhance user experience, manage sessions, and personalize the service. If we implement cookies, we will notify you and update this policy.

5. Sharing Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:
- If required by law or in response to a governmental request.
- To protect the rights, property, or safety of our service or other users.

6. Information Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information stored in our system but cannot guarantee absolute security. We recommend using secure passwords and keeping your personal information protected.

7. Data Retention

Data associated with your account and URLs will be retained while you actively use the service. If you wish to delete your account and associated data, you can contact us, and we will process the deletion.

8. User Rights

You have the right to:
- Request access to the data we hold about you.
- Request deletion of your data.
- Correct or update any incorrect data.

9. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you via email or by posting a prominent notice on our website.
